The variables of the tidy dataset, which is the output of the run_analysis.R script.

Variables 3-81 are the measurement means for each "subject" and "activity name" combination (Variables 1 and 2). 

For example, "tbodyaccelerometerstdy" is the mean of the measurements tbodyaccelerometerstdy from the merged train and test datasets (see https://d396qusza40orc.cloudfront.net/getdata%2Fprojectfiles%2FUCI%20HAR%20Dataset.zip for more details)
